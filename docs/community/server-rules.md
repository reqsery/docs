# Server Rules

These rules apply to the [Revro Discord server](https://discord.gg/vV2USr9phF). All members are expected to follow them.

---

## Rules

### 1. Be Respectful
Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.

### 2. Stay On Topic
Keep conversations relevant to the channel you're in. Use the correct channels for questions, showcases, and off-topic chat.

### 3. No Spam
Do not send repeated messages, flood channels with identical content, or use excessive mentions (@everyone, @here without reason).

### 4. No Advertising
Do not advertise other Discord servers, products, or services without permission from a moderator.

### 5. No NSFW Content
All content must be appropriate for a general audience. No adult, graphic, or disturbing content of any kind.

### 6. English Only in Main Channels
Please use English in main channels so the team and community can help you effectively. Language-specific channels may be added in the future.

### 7. No Piracy or Malware
Do not share cracked software, stolen assets, or any files that may contain malware.

### 8. Follow Discord's Terms of Service
All members must comply with [Discord's Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).

---

## Consequences

Violations may result in:
- A warning
- A temporary mute
- A kick from the server
- A permanent ban

The severity depends on the nature and frequency of the violation. Moderation decisions are made by the staff team and are final.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you see a rule violation:
- Right-click the message and click **Report** in Discord
- Or open a ticket in **#support** to alert the moderation team
- Do not engage with or escalate the situation yourself

---

## Questions?

If you're unsure whether something is allowed, ask in a support ticket before posting. The team is happy to clarify.
