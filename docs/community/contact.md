## What to Include in Your Message

For support requests, include as much of the following as you can:

- **Your account email** (for account/billing issues)
- **Your plan** (Free / Starter / Pro / Studio)
- **What you were trying to do**
- **Exact error message** (copy/paste, not a screenshot if possible)
- **When it started happening**
- **Steps you've already tried**

This helps us resolve your issue faster without back-and-forth.
