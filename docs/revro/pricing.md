# Pricing

Revro offers four plans ranging from a free tier to a professional studio tier. All plans include access to Roblox script generation, UI creation, and Discord server setup.

---

## Plans

### Monthly Billing

| | Free | Starter | Pro | Studio |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $0/mo | $10/mo | $20/mo | $50/mo |
| **Credits/month** | 25 | 150 | 500 | 1,500 |
| **Images/month** | — | — | 50 | 150 |
| **AI Model** | Standard | Advanced | Premium | Premium |
| **Support** | Community | Email | Priority | Early Access |
| **Chat History** | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

### Annual Billing *(Save up to 20%)*

| | Starter | Pro | Studio |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $9/mo | $17/mo | $40/mo |
| **Billed as** | $108/yr | $204/yr | $480/yr |

:::tip Annual savings
Annual billing locks in the discounted rate for a full year. You can cancel at any time — your plan stays active until the end of the billing period.
:::

## Feature Details by Plan

### Free
- 25 credits per month (resets monthly)
- Standard AI model (Claude Sonnet 4.5)
- Access to Roblox script generation and Discord builder
- Community support via Discord
- No chat history

### Starter
- 150 credits per month
- Advanced AI model (Claude Sonnet 4.6)
- Saved chat history
- Email support

### Pro *(Recommended)*
- 500 credits per month
- **50 AI-generated images per month**
- Premium AI model (Claude Opus 4.6)
- Priority support

### Studio
- 1,500 credits per month
- **150 AI-generated images per month**
- Premium AI model (Claude Opus 4.6)
- Early access to new features

| Small | 50 | $5 | $0.10 |

## Payments

Revro payments are processed securely by **[Lemon Squeezy](https://lemonsqueezy.com)**. Accepted payment methods include credit/debit cards and PayPal (where available).

You can manage your payment method, view invoices, and cancel your subscription from the **Billing** section of your Dashboard or via the customer portal link provided after checkout.

---

## Cancellation & Refunds

- You can cancel at any time from the Dashboard
- After cancellation, your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period
- You are then moved to the Free plan
- Refunds are evaluated case-by-case — contact [support@revro.dev](mailto:support@revro.dev)

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do unused credits roll over?**
No. Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. See [Credit System](/revro/credit-system) for details.

**Can I switch plans mid-cycle?**
Yes. Upgrading takes effect immediately. Downgrading takes effect at the next billing cycle.

**Is the annual plan locked in?**
The annual plan is billed as 12 monthly payments at the discounted rate. You can cancel before the next month to avoid the next charge.
